Rules to Participate in the Instagram Game

  • How to Participate: Simply comment on posts shared on our Instagram account. Your activity will be automatically tracked.
  • Categories: Compete in multiple categories:
    • Top Fastest Contributors: Be among the first to comment on new posts.
    • Top Most Consistent Contributors: Engage regularly over time.
    • Top Most Frequent Contributors: Comment the most across all posts.
    • Top Most Verbose: Write detailed and meaningful comments.
  • Posting Schedule: New posts are shared every day. Sometimes multiple times per day. So be sure to check in regularly to stay engaged. It is useful that you use instagram notifications and subscribe to post updates on our profile.
  • Data Collection: All data is gathered from the comment section of our Instagram posts. Ensure your comments align with our community guidelines.

How Scoring Works

Points are awarded based on your activity in each category:

  • Fastest Contributors: Earn points for being one of the first three commenters on a post.
  • Consistent Contributors: Points are given for commenting consistently over multiple posts.
  • Frequent Contributors: The more you comment, the higher your score in this category.
  • Verbose Comments: Longer, thoughtful comments are rewarded with higher points.

Score Announcements

Scores are announced 3 to 4 days after the week has concluded. This allows all participants time to see the posts and submit their answers for the quiz. The scores will always reflect the previous week's activity.

Keep engaging and climb the leaderboard! Winners will be announced weekly, so stay tuned for updates.
